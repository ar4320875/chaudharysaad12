About The Project



Zaibo is an Urdu cartoon series broadcasted on local
TV channels and YouTube Channel. It is accompanied
with Zaibo Monthly Magazine, Zaibo Android App
and Zaibo Print Campaign. The purpose of this project
is to make children learn the possible ways of avoiding
violence (Child Abuse) and torture which can be physical,
emotional or mental. The cartoon series consists of a
strong positive girl character, few supporting roles and
a strong storyline.
